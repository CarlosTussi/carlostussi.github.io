About

Computer Science graduate, with a passion for data and experience with customer service, I am currently expanding my expertises, pursuing an MSc in Data Science and AI in Sophia Antipolis, France.

As a former international steward for a company in the Middle East, I had the opportunity to live and work in a multicultural environment, having the chance to interact with people from different cultures and nationalities from all over the world. In adition to that, I have already had the chance to live for longer periods of time in Brazil, China, UAE, Italy and now France.

After acquiring crucial soft skills such as cultural awareness, adaptability and the ability to work with people with different backgrounds, I decided to enrich my knowledge, continuing my studies in the technology field with an MSc in the domain of data science and artificial inteligence.


We can communicate in Portuguese, English, French, Italian and Mandarin.
